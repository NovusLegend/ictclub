Features

The ICT Club provides a well-rounded platform for students to enhance their technical and creative skills in programming, video editing, and graphic design while preparing for future careers in technology and media.

  • Programming

  • Video Editing

  • Graphics design

Programming

Introduction to emerging fields like artificial intelligence, machine learning, and automation.

  • Hands-on coding sessions covering various languages such as Python, Java, C++, and JavaScript.
  • Workshops on software development, mobile app creation, and web development.
  • Algorithm challenges, coding competitions, and hackathons to enhance problem-solving skills.

Video Editing

Training on professional editing software like Adobe Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, and DaVinci Resolve.

  • Techniques in video transitions, color grading, effects, and audio synchronization.
  • Hands-on projects, including short films, documentaries, and promotional videos.
  • Collaboration with other clubs or school departments to create media content.
  • Opportunities to participate in video production competitions and content creation projects.

Graphics Design

  • Introduction to design principles, typography, color theory, and branding.
  • Hands-on training with tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, and CorelDRAW.
  • Designing posters, banners, logos, and social media content for real-world applications.

Special sessions on UI/UX design for web and app development.

Hands-on Training Sessions

Learn programming, video editing, and graphic design through practical lessons. Access industry-standard software and tools for real-world experience.

. Competitions and Hackathons

Participate in coding challenges, design contests, and video editing battles. Showcase your skills, win prizes, and gain recognition.

Expert-Led Workshops

Learn from ICT professionals and guest speakers in the tech industry. Gain insights into career opportunities and emerging technologies.

Innovation and Project Development

Work on real-world projects like apps, websites, and digital content. Solve community problems through technology-driven solutions.

Have a question? Check out the FAQ

Here are some of the questions and answers of some questions we ave otenly been asked

Who can join the ICT Club?

Anyone interested in technology, programming, video editing, or graphic design can join. No prior experience is required!

Do I need my own laptop to participate?

While having a personal laptop is beneficial, it is not mandatory. The club provides access to computers and necessary software during sessions.

What skills can I learn in the club?

You can learn programming, website development, graphic design, video editing, cybersecurity basics, and much more.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, a small subscription fee is required to access training, workshops, and club resources. Fees vary based on membership type.

Do members receive certificates?

Yes! Members who complete training programs and workshops receive certificates to boost their portfolios and careers.

Can I join if I am a beginner with no ICT knowledge?

Absolutely! We offer beginner-friendly sessions and mentorship programs to help you learn from scratch.
